Compiling The Spox chain of Programs
------------------------------------

You need Microsoft Community Visual Studio 2013 (Free) to Compile the progs.


- The arduino  Part has been compiled (within MS C VS2013) with Arduino 1.61 (Free)

- Other modules are in VBNET with a Object Oriented (partial) Approach.

- The installer is compiled with Innosetup (Free)
